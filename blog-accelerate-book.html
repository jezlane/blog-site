Slide Notes on the Book, Accelerate by Forsgren, Humble and Kim

Published April 1st, 20234 min read

At the beginning of the year I read Accelerate by Forsgren, Humble and Kim. And over the past few months I have found myself studying the book more and more. I thought I would post my notes on each chapter. The ideas in the book seem simple, yet they are far from easy. Hope people find this useful, let me know in the comments below. Thanks

Chapter 1 - Accelerate.

Key Takeaway: Work in small teams, with short cycles, is a business differentiator, because it increases customer satisfaction.

Chapter 2 - Measuring Performance.

Key Takeaway: Measure and manage to the 4 deployment measurements to improve overall performance

Chapter 3 - Measuring and Changing Culture.

Key Takeaway: Culture is really all about Trust, build trust for a better culture!

Chapter 4 - Measuring and Changing Culture.

Key Takeaway: Continuous development and continuous delivery leads to less pain and burnout; and higher quality!
Download Slide

Chapter 5 - Architecture.

Key Takeaway: Loosely coupled Architecture enables experimentation and scaling
Download Slide

Chapter 6 - Integrating InfoSec into the Delivery Lifecycle.

Key Takeaway: Security is everyone to build into the service, to illustrate the authors talk to the benefits of the "Rugged Manifesto"
Download Slide

Chapter 7 - Management Practices for Software.

Key Takeaway: Lean Management practices include limiting Work in progress; providing a visual displays of that progress; getting feedback from production and minimizing the approval process to make changes
Download Slide

Chapter 8 - Product Development.

Key Takeaway: Real Lean Management practices, rather than just Agile Practices, lead to a virtuous cycle of learning and rapid product innovation
Download Slide

Chapter 9 - Making Work Sustainable.

Key Takeaway: Reduce deployment pain by making it easier to push to production and foster failures to become sources of learning rather than blame!
Download Slide

Chapter 10 - Making Work Sustainable.

Key Takeaway: Measure employee engagement with Employee Net Promoter Score (eNPS) and focus on Continuous Delivery and Lean Practices to improve engagement.
Download Slide

Chapter 11 - Leaders and Managers.

Key Takeaway: Leaders need to focus on Inspiring and Motivating others and creating a high Trust environment. Managers need to connect the organizations strategy to the work of their teams, whilst creating a safe and growth focused environment.
